I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the [School of Integrated Circuits, Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn), supervised by Prof. Lecheng Ruan and Prof. Qining Wang. Before that, I received my M.Sc. in Computer Science (with Distinction) from the University of Bristol in 2020, and my B.Eng. in Intelligence Science and Technology from Nankai University in 2018.

My research focuses on the intersection of computer vision, robotics, and wearable systems, with an emphasis on SLAM, 3D reconstruction, and locomotion mode prediction for assistive devices.

Research Interests
======
- Computer Vision
- Robotics & SLAM
- 3D Reconstruction
- Bipedal Locomotion
- Wearable Assistive Devices

Recent Highlights
======
- Published in **IEEE TNSRE** on learning-free locomotion mode prediction using terrain reconstruction and visual-inertial odometry
- Senior Research Engineer at the National Key Lab for General Artificial Intelligence, BIGAI (2021–2024)
- Awarded **Engineer of the Year** at BIGAI
